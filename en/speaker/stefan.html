Stefan Wehrmeyer

Stefan Wehrmeyer is a data journalist at the investigative office CORRECT!V. Since 2009 he promotes Open Data Nahverkehr and started the Freedom of Information (FOI) portal FragDenStaat.de in 2009.

Together with Friedrich Lindenberg, Stefan will hold a workshop on 29.04.2017.
