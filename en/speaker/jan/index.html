Jan Lutz

Jan Lutz founded the „Büro für Gestalten“ in 2007 in Stuttgart. It started as a communications office focused on public relations for companies with an emphasis on sustainability. Today the focus lies on developing new strategies and counseling for sustainable mobility.

In 2015 he started being the Lab Lead of Open Knowledge Labs Stuttgart, a local group focusing on the fine dust measuring project luftdaten.info.

Jan will show you how to build a fine dust sensor on 29.04.2017.
