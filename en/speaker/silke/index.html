Silke Ramelow

Silke Ramelow is chairwoman of the board of BildungsCent e.v. a charitable organization she founded in 2003. Their focus is to support schools in including topics of societal change in their curricula. It aims at enabling students to participate more in their everyday environment. Together with Datenschule BildungsCent has recently launched the project jedeschule.de.

Silke will hold a workshop on 29.04.2017 together with Helene Hahn and Moritz Neujeffski regarding educational data.
